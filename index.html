About Me

Hello there! I'm a web developer and designer based in San Francisco Bay Area.

Having spent a large chunk of my life in arts and music, I've finally found my passion at the web design and technology. I'm an exceptionally creative and dependable entry Level Web Developer with a stellar customer service record and superb work ethic. Broadly and deeply knowledgeable in a wide variety of computer languages as well as the principles and techniques of website construction and maintenance. Highly adept at conveying complex technical information to a variety of professional and lay audiences in a clear and understandable manner.

Currently, I spend most of my time designing websites and participating in the #100DaysOfCode challenge to keep my skills sharp. When I'm not busy, you can find me obsessing over food and cats, or working on one of my many hobbies like fashion blogging and traveling. I tend to switch things up every few months, so if you want to know what I'm up to, just follow me! :)
